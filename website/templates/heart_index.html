Heart Disease

Overview

Heart disease encompasses various conditions impacting the heart. These include:

  • Coronary artery disease affecting blood vessels
  • Arrhythmias, or irregular heart rhythms
  • Congenital heart defects present at birth
  • Conditions involving heart valves
  • Diseases of the heart muscle
  • Infections of the heart
  • A significant number of heart diseases can be averted or managed through choices promoting a healthy lifestyle.

Symptoms

Typical indicators of a heart attack encompass:

  • A feeling of pressure, constriction, discomfort, or a squeezing or throbbing feeling in your chest or arms, which might extend to your neck, jaw, or back
  • Nausea, upset stomach, heartburn, or stomach pain
  • Difficulty in breathing
  • A cold, clammy sweat
  • Extreme tiredness
  • Feeling lightheaded or experiencing abrupt bouts of dizziness
